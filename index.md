Adam is a current Master of Information Technology student at the University of Melbourne, specialising in Artificial Intelligence. He is working on a research project in machine learning fairness under the supervision of Jean Honorio.

His background spans software engineering, psychology, and music, giving him a scientific and human-centred approach to technical work. Outside of technology, Adam is a classical pianist who performs and teaches regularly around Melbourne.
